VISHALNAGARI – THE INTELLIGENT & SUSTAINABLE CITY PORTFOLIO

Course Overview

Algorithms are the engines that power every intelligent system—from route optimization and resource allocation to dynamic planning and sustainable city management. Understanding algorithms allows us to build cities that are **faster, smarter, and more efficient**. In this project, algorithmic thinking helps solve real-world problems such as:

• Efficient distribution of civic resources • Optimal traffic routing • Sustainable agriculture planning • Industrial workflow optimization • Fair allocation in governance

This project demonstrates how algorithmic design can shape the future of modern smart cities like **Vishalnagari**.

Our Vision

Vishalnagari is a **one-of-a-kind city** — a living, intelligent ecosystem where sustainability meets innovation. Known as India’s capital of civic heritage, it honors centuries of tradition while embracing technology. The city exclusively uses **100% electric vehicles**, ensuring a zero-emission, future-ready environment.

With **smart infrastructure**, algorithm-driven systems harmonize daily life:
• Traffic flows smoothly through optimized paths • Water and energy resources are distributed efficiently • Agriculture thrives through intelligent irrigation models • Governance decisions rely on data-driven fairness

Vishalnagari proves that **technology + sustainability = a resilient future**.

Business Ideas (Based on Algorithms)

• Smart Traffic Optimization System using Dijkstra's
• Automated Industrial Workflow Scheduler
• Digital Market Demand Prediction using Greedy Strategies
• Irrigation Optimization using Dynamic Programming
• Civic Resource Allocation using Graph Matching
