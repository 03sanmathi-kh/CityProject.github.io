Project Overview

Vishalnagari is a smart city where only electric vehicles move, creating a clean and quiet environment. Charging stations line its boulevards, and intelligent traffic systems keep journeys smooth. Public transport runs on autonomous electric buses and trams, connecting every corner efficiently. The commercial sector buzzes with smart marketplaces, the industrial zone thrives on clean logistics, and the agricultural fields use modern irrigation for sustainable farming. Automated waste and water systems protect the environment, giving citizens fresh air and a healthier lifestyle. Vishalnagari shines as a living model of innovation, proving that technology and sustainability can grow together.
